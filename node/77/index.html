Welcome to Our Site

The Chinese Taoist Martial Arts Association (CTMAA) is a school for those pursuing authentic, in-depth, and thorough Chinese internal martial arts training.   If you'd like to have a quick tour on what we teach at CTMAA, you can click Video Gallery of the Chinese Taoist Martial Arts Association to watch the videos first.   Feel free to visit the school during our class hours.  

If you have some experiences on internal martial arts, you will definitely realize what you have missed in your previous training after you join us.  If you are new to the internal martial arts, you will appreciate how lucky you are to be able to begin your training with the best methods available and be on the right track toward the goal of acquiring the genuine internal arts after you become a student of CTMAA.   If you have a quick question, you can always contact us via phone.



The CTMAA is closed. Check the home page for more information.
