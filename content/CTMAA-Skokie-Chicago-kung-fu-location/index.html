The school, 2,700 square feet, is located in downtown (near the intersection of Oakton Street and Lincoln Avenue), Skokie, a northern suburb of Chicago

Spacious classroom in a safe area 

 

Directions to the school if you take I-94 W:

1.  Take EXIT 39B toward EAST TOUHY AVE.

2.  Turn LEFT onto SKOKIE BLVD.

3.  Turn LEFT onto OAKTON ST.

4.  5039 OAKTON ST.  (on the left side of the street)

 

Directions to the school if you take I-94 E:

1.  Merge onto IL-58 E/DEMPSTER ST via EXIT 37B.

2.  Turn RIGHT onto SKOKIE BLVD.

3.  Turn RIGHT onto OAKTON ST.

4.  5039 OAKTON ST. (on the left side of the street)

 

Directions to the school from Chicago if you take public transportation (CTA 'L'):

1.  Take the RED line or PURPLE line towards HOWARD.

2.  Transfer to the YELLOW line towards SKOKIE at the HOWARD station.

3.  Arrive at the OAKTON-SKOKIE station.

4.  Walk WEST for 0.4 miles (about 10 minutes).

5.  5039 OAKTON ST. (on the left side of the street)
