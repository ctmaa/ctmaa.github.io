2015 YiZungYue 3rd Generation Disciples

Tue, 03/29/2016 - 15:21 — admin

 On June 28, 2015, Master Lin accepted eight of his students as the disciples to carry on the legacy of YiZungYue School martial arts.   They are (from right to left in the picture) Ji-Hoon Kim, Wayne Guccione, Martin Davids, Daniel Polotsky, John Ryan, Marc Fine, Nelia Janik, and Tom Pizur.    The YizungYue martial arts are based on the Pre-Heaven Power Method and the three major internal martial arts: Ba Gua, Hsing I, and Tai Chi.  The disciples were granted the title of YiZungYue 3rd generation lineage holder. The title includes the lineages of the Cheng School Gao Style Ba Gua (Ba Gua 8th generation), He Bei Style Hsing I (10th generation) and Yang Style Tai Chi (7th generation).
